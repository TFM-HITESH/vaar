# Code of Conduct

Envaar is committed to maintaining a respectful, focused and collaborative project environment.

## Expected Behavior

Contributors are expected to:

- communicate professionally and respectfully
- keep feedback specific and constructive
- focus on the code, the problem and the evidence
- respect privacy, boundaries and confidentiality
- make room for different levels of experience
- assume good faith unless there is a clear reason not to

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not acceptable in this project:

- harassment, threats, intimidation or stalking
- discriminatory language or conduct
- sexualized language or conduct in project spaces
- public or repeated attempts to shame, harass or pressure others
- doxxing, privacy violations or unauthorized disclosure of sensitive information
- deliberate disruption of conversations, reviews or collaboration

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to:

- issues and pull requests
- code review comments
- discussions and any project-hosted community spaces
- private project communications related to Envaar

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may take reasonable action in response to conduct that violates this policy, including:

- removing or editing comments
- issuing a warning
- limiting participation in project spaces
- closing issues or pull requests
- removing access where necessary
- banning and blocking bad actors

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to the Envaar maintainers through the project’s private contact channel.

If the concern also involves sensitive data, secret exposure or unsafe output, follow [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md) as well.
